About Me

Hi, my name is David, I'm an aerospace engineer at Northrop Grumman. I mostly do model based systems engineering (MBSE), with a focus on data and functional architecture. I got my B.S from UCLA in 2019, and my M.S from the University of Michigan in 2021, both in aerospace engineering and with a focus on autonomous vehicles and optimization. For my masters program, I explored different image processing and segmentation techniques on ultrasonic c-scans of composite laminate panels for impact damage analysis. You can check out that paper here!

While at UCLA I was very interested in space propulsion & systems engineering, but towards the end of my time at Michigan, I became interested in AI & machine learning, which is mostly what I write about now.

Outside of STEM, I spend a lot of time studying piano & music theory. Also, I draw! Check out my instagram.

Please bear with me as I continue to add features! To-do-list: hyperlinks, more pictures, fix equations.
