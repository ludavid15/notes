About Me

Hi, my name is David, I'm an aerospace engineer at Northrop Grumman where I do model based systems engineering (MBSE). I got my B.S from UCLA in 2019, and my M.S from the University of Michigan in 2021, both in aerospace engineering, and the latter with a focus on autonomous vehicles and optimization. My master's thesis was an exploration of image processing and segmentation on ultrasonic scans of composite laminate panels for impact damage analysis.

I'm curious about a lot of things, but at the moment, I'm thinking mostly about space systems engineering, agile, music theory (mostly jazz), and machine learning.
