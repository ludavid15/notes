Welcome!

This is my personal website where I document things I have learned. Some of these are a direct copy of my notes from school, while others are more thought out like a blog post. The purpose of this site is twofold - both as a way for me to remember things, and as a place for me to write about new topics to help my learning.

About Me

Hi, my name is David, I'm a systems engineer at Northrop Grumman where I model and design satellite architectures. I have both a B.S and M.S in aerospace engineering, from UCLA in 2019 and the University of Michigan in 2021 respectively (with a focus on autonomous vehicles and optimization). My master's thesis was an exploration of image processing and segmentation on ultrasonic scans of composite laminate panels for impact damage analysis.

I'm curious about a lot of things, but at the moment, I'm thinking mostly about space systems engineering, agile, music theory (mostly jazz related), and machine learning.
